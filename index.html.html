WELCOME TO NDEJJE UNIVERSITY

Student Games

By Patricia Lodio

Ndejje University is one of the leading private contributors to Ugandan Athletics development. We have sent out athletes to compete for both University and country at regional, continental and international championships. The university sports and recreation department is blessed with a dedicated, professional and winning team of coaches.

The games include: Track and Field Athletics, Mini-Marathon, Badminton, Swimming, Basketball, Table Tennis, Chess, Tennis, Cross-country, Volleyball, Football, Wood ball, Rugby, Netball, and Handball.

Academics

Academics is one of the primary reason one joins the university. To equip students with our Academic skills,we have a series of activities inclusive of lectures, assessments,study travels,internship placements ,workshps and seminars,and research

Detailed information regarding academic activities can be obtained from your faculty administrators' office. This is inclusive of study timetables for all programs; Day,Evening,Weekend and In-service
